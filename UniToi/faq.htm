Frequently Asked Question

Q1: What is UniToi?


UniToi is a website that can help you find toilets all around the University of Sydney. Using UniToi, you can locate the toilet nearest to you, or you can also find toilets that matches your needs.

Q2: Who can Use UniToi?


Anybody can use UniToi, as long as they are around the area of University of Sydney. Whether you are a university staff, student, or visitor, you can use UniToi to find toilets all around the university.

Q3: How do I Find a Toilet?


You can use the "Quick Search" function on the Home page in order to find the nearest toilet without any filters for the toilet. You can also go to the Search page to find a toilet with applied filters in order to find a toilet that suits you! Clicking on the toilet from the search results will take you to that toilet's page, which contains more details about the toilets, including reviews left by others who have used it before.

Q4: Do I Need to Log In To Find a Toilet?


No! You can search for a toilet without having to log in. Logging in will enable you to review and report toilets.

Q5: What is Quick Search?


Quick search is a function that will instantly show you the toilet nearest to you. To use this function, simply head to our home page and press the 'Quick Search' button.

Q6: How do I Review a Toilet?


Any user, without logging in, can use the find toilet feature, but to leave reviews and react to other reviews, you will need to sign up and be logged in to your account. Only then can you review a toilet and interact with other reviews, such as leaving likes and comments. To review a toilet, find the toilet you want to review using the 'Search' function and scroll down to the review section.

Q7: How do I Report a Toilet?


There are two ways to report a toilet. The first way is by clicking the 'Report' button on the navigation bar. You will then have to choose which toilet to report and fill in the report form. Another way is by using the 'Search' function, find the toilet to report, and then scroll down to the 'Report' section, where you can a link to the report form page.
